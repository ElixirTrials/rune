## What This Article Covers

This article presents the theoretical foundation and algorithmic design of Rune's
parametric episodic memory approach. It surveys composable weight-space memory
strategies, specifies the Doc-to-LoRA trajectory extension and Evolution Operator,
proposes an experimental design for empirical validation, and discusses expected
contributions and limitations honestly in terms of claim tier.
